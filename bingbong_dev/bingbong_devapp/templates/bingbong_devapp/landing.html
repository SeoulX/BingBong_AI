Introducing BingBong AI

We’ve trained a model called BingBong AI, a simple AI chatbot powered by Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques, specifically Text Classification and Sentiment Analysis.

BingBong AI interacts in a conversational way. The dialogue format makes it possible for BingBong to answer follow-up questions, admit its mistakes, challenge incorrect premises, and reject inappropriate requests.

This repository contains the source code for BingBong AI.

Features

Sentiment Analysis: BingBong can identify the sentiment (positive, negative, neutral) of user input, enabling it to adjust its responses accordingly.

Usage

BingBong AI is designed to be a simple and interactive chatbot. You can interact with it by typing your messages in the terminal window. Based on the text classification and sentiment analysis, BingBong will generate appropriate responses.

We are excited to introduce BingBong AI to get users’ feedback and learn about its strengths and weaknesses. During the research preview, usage of BingBong AI is free. Try it now!
